Lemon Garlic Pasta

A bright, weeknight-friendly pasta inspired by Mediterranean flavors. It comes together in under 20 minutes and pairs well with grilled chicken or a crisp salad.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti (or linguine)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter (optional for richness)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste (optional)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, warm olive oil in a pan over low heat. Add garlic and gently cook until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Stir in lemon zest and a pinch of red pepper flakes; remove from heat.
  4. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain pasta. Add pasta to the pan with the garlic oil.
  5. Add lemon juice, butter (if using), and a splash of pasta water. Toss until glossy and well coated.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper. Add Parmesan if desired and toss again, adjusting with more pasta water as needed.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
